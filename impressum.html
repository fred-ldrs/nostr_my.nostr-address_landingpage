What is NOSTR?

NOSTR is an open protocol that enables censorship-resistant and decentralized social networking. It allows anyone to publish and interact without relying on a central authority.

What is a NOSTR address?

A NOSTR address is a unique identifier (npub) that represents your public profile on the NOSTR network. It is derived from a cryptographic key pair, ensuring privacy and security.
